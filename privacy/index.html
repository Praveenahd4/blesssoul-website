Introduction

BlessSoul ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by Praveena Hosuru Dinesh Bhat, a sole proprietor and individual developer based in Shivamogga, Karnataka, India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information within the constraints of a one-person operation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your data when you use the BlessSoul mobile application (the "App").

By using BlessSoul, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Solo Developer Context

CRITICAL DISCLOSURE: BlessSoul is operated by one individual developer with limited resources. This affects our privacy practices:

  • Best-effort compliance: We strive to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations but have limited resources compared to large companies
  • Reasonable accommodations: Privacy rights requests will be honored within reasonable timeframes (typically 30-45 days, may extend to 60 days for complex requests)
  • No dedicated privacy team: Privacy inquiries are handled by the sole developer alongside development, support, and all other operations
  • Limited infrastructure: Data protection measures reflect solo developer resources, not enterprise-level systems
  • Good-faith efforts: We make good-faith efforts to protect your privacy and honor your rights within our capabilities

We take your privacy seriously, but please understand the limitations inherent in a one-person operation.

1. Information We Collect

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Data Storage Locations

Supabase (Primary Cloud Database):

  • What's stored: Account information, goals, schedules, reminders, manifestations, subscription tracking
  • Location: United States (Google Cloud Platform infrastructure)
  • Security: Row-level security, PostgreSQL database, HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Apple iCloud (CloudKit) - Optional:

  • What's stored: Synced copy of your goals, schedules, reminders, manifestations
  • Control: You control whether iCloud sync is enabled
  • Security: Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple

Local Device Storage (CoreData):

  • What's stored: Encrypted diary entries (AES-256-GCM encryption), cached data for offline access
  • Security: iOS app sandbox, encrypted storage

3.2 Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data uses HTTPS/TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Diary entries use AES-256-GCM encryption (military-grade)
  • Password Security: bcrypt hashing with salt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Controls: Row-level security, API authentication, rate limiting
  • Biometric Protection: Optional Face ID/Touch ID for diary access (processed locally by iOS)

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data as long as your account is active:

  • During trial (7 days): Full data retention
  • Active subscription: Full data retention and sync
  • Grace period (30 days post-expiration): Read-only data retention
  • Day 30 post-expiration: Automatic deletion

7.2 Manual Account Deletion

When you manually delete your account:

  • Immediate: All user content deleted from database, authentication terminated
  • Within 24-48 hours: CloudKit data fully removed
  • Within 30 days: Backups purged, residual logs anonymized

Important: Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Data cannot be recovered.

7.3 Automatic Account Deletion

If subscription expires and is not renewed:

  • Days 0-29: Account in read-only mode, all data retained
  • Day 30: Account automatically and permanently deleted
  • Prevention: Resubscribe anytime before day 30 to restore full access
